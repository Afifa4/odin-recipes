Toast

Description

Toast is a simple breakfast dish made by browning slices of bread in a toaster or on a skillet. It's often served with butter, jam, or other spreads and can be enjoyed as a quick and easy meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Butter
  • jam
  • other spreads (optional)

Steps

  1. Place bread slices in a toaster and toast until golden brown.
  2. Spread butter, jam, or your preferred topping on the toast.
  3. Serve warm.
