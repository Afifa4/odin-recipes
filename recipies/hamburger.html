Hamburger

Description

A Hamburger is a classic and juicy sandwich made with a seasoned beef patty, served in a bun, and often topped with lettuce, tomato, cheese, and condiments. It's a satisfying and easy-to-make meal that's perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 hamburger buns
  • 2 slices of cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise (optional)
  • Pickles (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Season the ground beef with salt and pepper, then form it into two
  3. Grill or cook the patties in the skillet for about 4-5 minutes per side,until fully cooked.
  4. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute ofcooking to melt.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in the skillet.
  6. Assemble the hamburger by placing the patty on the bun, then addinglettuce, tomato, and any other desired toppings.
  7. Serve hot with your choice of condiments.
