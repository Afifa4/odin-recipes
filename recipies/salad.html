Salad

Description

A salad is a refreshing dish made from a variety of fresh vegetables, often tossed with a light dressing. Itâ€™s a healthy option that can be enjoyed as a side or a main dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, etc.)
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • Salad dressing (optional)

Steps

  1. Combine mixed greens, cucumber, tomato, and carrots in a bowl.
  2. Drizzle with your favorite salad dressing.
  3. Toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve immediately.
